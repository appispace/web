Appistories

Share stories, moments, thoughts, acts, feelings – anything really – that brings you joy. These can be from the simplest of things to life changing events.

With 15 joyful categories to choose from you can spread joy in many ways. Follow all or select your most joyful ones and your feed will be full of posts that interest you the most.

Using up to 500 characters and 4 media files you can share your joys with others, leaving them feeling more joyful with every swipe.

Sharing these experiences allows you to brighten someone’s day, inspire someone to do something kind or see how many people have shared in a similar kind of joy.

Show yourself, and others, how much genuine goodness we have all around us.

Appicards

Some days you want a little direction or inspiration and need some guidance. With over 77 cards to choose from see what shuffling the deck brings to you.

Whatever your Appi word may be, see how you can incorporate it into your day in a positive way.

It is a sign? A wisdom you need to hear? A direction to help you make a decision? or just some fun!

Regardless, be sure to pick a card daily and find out what the cards say to you!

Perspective

Ever need a positive spin on something not so great? Post your situation and have others share their encouraging perspectives. There are many sides to a story and maybe you just need to see the situation through different eyes. Your story is public but the comments are not, so take the ones you find helpful and leave the rest.

This is the only place with comments so you must opt in. We support a conscious choice about opening yourself up to feedback.

Gratitude Journal

A fantastic way to realize the good already in your life is to write it down. Therefore, we have a private space to keep track of what you are grateful for every day. The more you write down, the more you’ll start to notice and be able to focus on what really matters to you and what brings you joy.

When you’re not so sure, look back on old entries to remind yourself of what you have and appreciate most in your life.

Crappispace

We know not every day or every moment is a good one. We all have hardships and sufferings, that’s just part of life. Unfortunately, we often keep these thoughts and feelings inside. So Crappispace is a private page all about letting those feelings out.

Once you let them out, it helps to let them go.

Get it off your chest and out of your mind, so you can return to focusing on the good things around you.

Origin Story

Appispace started when the Founder was trying to find her passion. After hearing “what makes you happy?” over and over again, she began to seriously think about the question. She reflected on moments she was happiest, or those around her were happiest, and that’s when it struck. It was often the little gestures, simple acts, kind words, and small moments creating life’s most joyful memories.

She realized that every day we are surrounded by so many good things, but they are often overlooked, disregarded or thought of as insignificant. People are more likely to notice the not-so-great things; share negative experiences and think about what didn’t go right, instead of the opposite.

She recognized if people started paying attention to those simple little things, they would add up. They would notice more. They would see how much good exists in our everyday. And if people not only started noticing more but started sharing these simple things; then more people would notice them too.

Appispace was created to provide a dedicated platform for sharing simple acts, simple joys and simple pleasures. By sharing these people begin to find new joys, see more goodness and overall spread more joy to the world.
